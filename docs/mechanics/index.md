# Game Mechanics

Custom rules and systems for the World of LOR campaigns.

---

## Bankai & Domain System

- [Full Bankai Rules](/docs/mechanics/bankai) - Complete system documentation

### Quick Reference

**What is Bankai?**
A custom power system inspired by anime, allowing characters to unlock devastating ultimate abilities tied to their soul.

**Unlock Requirements:**
- Automatic at Level 13
- OR earned through a major "feel something" moment in roleplay

**Usage:**
- Costs: Full Action + Bonus Action to activate
- Cooldown: 1d8 days between uses
- Duration: Varies by ability

### Bankai Tiers

| Tier | Level | Power |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Base** | 13 | Initial unlock |
| **Evolved** | 15 | Enhanced form |
| **Perfected** | 17 | Mastered form |
| **True** | 19+ | Ultimate form |

## House Rules

### Combat
- **Flanking:** +2 to hit (not advantage)
- **Massive Damage:** Instant death if single hit exceeds max HP
- **Death Saves:** Hidden from other players

### Rest
- **Short Rest:** 1 hour, spend Hit Dice
- **Long Rest:** 8 hours, full recovery (only in safe locations)
- **Exhaustion:** Gained from extended travel without rest

### Magic
- **Identify:** Requires the spell (no automatic identification)
- **Counterspell:** Must identify spell first (Arcana check)
- **Resurrection:** Increasingly difficult, requires quest components

## Plagas-Specific Rules

### Narcotics System
Frontier substances with mechanical effects and addiction risks.

### Survival
- **Heat/Humidity:** Constitution saves in jungle
- **Disease:** Increased risk in swamps
- **Provisions:** Strict tracking in wilderness

### Exploration
- **Hex Crawl:** 6-mile hexes, random encounters
- **Discovery:** Ruins, resources, and secrets to find
