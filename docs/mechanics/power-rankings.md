# Power Rankings

The ranking systems used across LOR to measure power.

## Faction Power Rankings

Power rankings for kingdoms and factions denote on a scale of **S, A, B, C, D** the combined military, economic, and diplomatic might at the disposal of a given faction.

| Tier | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **S** | Continental superpower with overwhelming combined might |
| **A** | Major regional power with significant resources |
| **B** | Notable power with respectable influence |
| **C** | Local power with limited reach |
| **D** | Minor faction with minimal influence |

## Heroic Rankings

Individual hero power levels based on experience and renown.

| Tier | Description | Character Levels |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| **S+** | Godlike beings of legendary renown | 21+ |
| **S** | Master heroes of legendary renown | 17-20 |
| **A** | Realm heroes of great renown | 14-16 |
| **B** | Realm heroes of some renown | 10-13 |
| **C** | Local heroes of great renown | 6-9 |
| **D** | Local heroes of little renown | 1-5 |
