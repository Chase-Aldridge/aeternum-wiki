## Description

One of the last open havens for pirates, explorers, freebooters, and sailors. The half-orc clans here are seeking peace after the extermination of their orc ancestors, and have accepted the exiled humans because of this shared suffering.

## Characteristics

- Treasure hunters and ancient ruins archaeologists flock here to congregate and mount seaborne expeditions
- Safe harbor for those rejected by other kingdoms

## Leadership

### Warchief Ka Orum
