## Description

A great city built upon ancient "Forebearer" jungle ruins. Vol'Anlek is home to the oldest race to inhabit the continent, controlling much of the northwest realms.

## Characteristics

- Masters of war, statecraft, and magic
- Slow to change traditions
- Mistrust outsiders
- Two major noble families who share power: **Alephor** and **Horun**

## Leadership

### Grand Hierophant Ludus Alephor

The current political and spiritual leader of the kingdom. Ludus Alephor is a ruthless and dogmatic archmage from the oldest and most religious house of the high elves.
