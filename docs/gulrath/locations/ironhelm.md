## Description

The second oldest people on the continent, masters of the entire mountainous southeast. Ironhelm is a collection of warrior, crafting, religious, and explorer great clans.

## Military Might

- Boasts the most elite troops
- Highest quality gear
- Most war machines among the great realms
- **Recent invention of gunpowder**
- Nearly exterminated the entire orc tribal holds in the Dragonspine Mountains

## Ongoing Conflicts

- Battle of attrition with their dark kin, the **duergar**, underneath their major holds
