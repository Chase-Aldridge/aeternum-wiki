## Description

The most powerful trader and merchant class on the continent. Their spycraft and ritual magic is also feared.

## Characteristics

- Deal with djinn and elemental lords from the Plane of Fire and the Plane of Earth
- **Wealth is power here**, completely determining the matriarchal line of succession
- Sponsor and patron to the famed Explorer's Association

## Current Situation

Recently come under brutal raids from the remaining wild elf bloodletters pushing out from The Blood Lands.
