## Description

A free trade city where sea elves are looked down upon by high elves. Trade fleets and mercenaries are highly prized here.

## Characteristics

- Rumored to be bought and paid for, and also in debt to Dragmar Aer
- Harbor for dungeon seekers, pirates, brigands, and master traders
- **Site of the Explorer's Association headquarters**
