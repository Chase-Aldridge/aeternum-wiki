## Description

One of the most religious places in the lands. Pilgrims and acolytes come here often to worship and seek guidance.

## Characteristics

- Peaceful and democratic city
- **Has suffered most during past conflicts** due to its peaceful nature
- The most eclectic mix of races and exiles on the mainland
- Defending with steep tariffs by the high elf kingdoms
- Site of many traditional rituals, festivals, and fertility rites

## Notable Features

- Prophets, seers, and clerics of some renown serve in the temples
- Center for religious pilgrimage across Gul'Rath
