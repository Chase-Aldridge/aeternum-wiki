# THE GOLDEN LEGION

*The Remnants of the Great Host*

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Population** | 44,000 |
| **Races** | High Elf |
| **Government** | Military Order |
| **Leader** | Commander Striker Horun |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |
| **Deity** | Ehlonna, Bahamut |
| **Power Ranking** | A Tier |

## History

THE GOLDEN LEGION is the remnants of the great host summoned to battle in THE RED CRUSADE. When the war ended, rather than disband, the elite forces remained unified under the command of Striker Horun.

## Military Strength

The Legion is renowned for:

### Training
- Highly trained in regimented war stratagems
- Strict discipline and hierarchy
- Years of combat experience from THE RED CRUSADE

### Composition
- **Fighters** - Core infantry and cavalry
- **Paladins** - Holy warriors
- **Clerics** - Battlefield healers and divine support
- **Rangers** - Scouts and skirmishers
- **Monks** - Elite martial artists

## House Horun

The Legion is ruled by the lesser noble HOUSE HORUN:
- Striker Horun leads both house and legion
- Though "lesser" in title, they command the most military power
- Political rival to HOUSE ALEPHOR

## Role in VOL'ANLEK

The Golden Legion serves as:
- The primary military force of the high elven kingdom
- Defenders against external threats
- A counterbalance to the magical power of HOUSE ALEPHOR
