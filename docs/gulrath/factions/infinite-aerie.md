## Description

A coalition of merchant lords, master traders, and winged plutocrats under the wealthy rule of their queen.

Responsible for master spies, emissaries, diplomats and traders across the whole continent. Their alliance with fire and earth djinn has made their military forces masters of fast attack tactics.
