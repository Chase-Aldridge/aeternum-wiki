## Description

Little is known of this power cabal centered in the mage high tower of CORMANDIR.

The current Grand Magister has been rumored to have conducted diabolical experiments to contact The Nine Hells and The Outer Realm. Trade and diplomacy is limited, and their magic comes at excruciating prices.
