## Description

The nomadic military remnants leftover after the end of **The Red Crusade** when their leader was defeated at the **Battle of Broken Blades**.

## Current Status

Currently nomadic bands of wild elf bloodletters are the primary danger to travelers through:
- The Aer Desert
- The Blood Lands

Their numbers and power base are unknown.
