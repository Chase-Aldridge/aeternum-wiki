## Description

The remnants of the great host summoned to battle in THE RED CRUSADE. Ruled over by the lesser noble HOUSE HORUN, led by their leader Striker Horun.

Highly trained in regimented war stratagems, using fighters, paladins, clerics, rangers and monks.
