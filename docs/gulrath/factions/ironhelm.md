# THE IRONHELM CONFEDERACY

*Masters of the Mountain Realm*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Population** | 500,000 |
| **Races** | Mountain/Hill Dwarves |
| **Government** | Imperial Confederacy |
| **Leader** | Emperor Aldrick Ironshield |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Deity** | Grungnar |
| **Power Ranking** | S Tier |

## Continental Superpower

IRONHELM is the most powerful faction on Gul'Rath, controlling the entire mountainous southeast. Their combination of elite troops, master craftsmen, and technological innovation makes them unmatched.

## Military Might

The dwarves possess:

### Elite Forces
- Most elite troops on the continent
- Highest quality gear forged by master smiths
- War machines including siege engines
- **Gunpowder** - A terrifying recent invention

### Victories
- Nearly exterminated the orc tribal holds
- Ongoing war of attrition with the duergar
- Stood firm in THE RED CRUSADE

## Major Clans

### Clan Ironshield (100,000)
- The Emperor's clan
- Peerless warriors and smiths
- Magical armaments and artifacts

### Clan Dragonhelm (300,000)
- Led by King Huren Dragonhelm
- Masters of rune magic
- Control "The Shadowforge"

## Economy

IRONHELM's wealth comes from:
- Master craftsmanship
- Mining operations
- Trade in weapons and armor
- Technological innovation

## The Duergar Threat

Beneath the mountains, war never ends:
- Dark dwarves constantly probe defenses
- Resources committed to eternal struggle
- The surface world's fate may depend on this hidden war
