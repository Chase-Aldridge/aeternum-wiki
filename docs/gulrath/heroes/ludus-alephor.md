*Archmage and Spiritual Leader of the High Elves*

## Background

Ludus Alephor is a ruthless and dogmatic archmage who serves as both the political and spiritual leader of VOL'ANLEK. As head of the oldest and most religious house of the high elves, he wields immense power through hereditary bloodlines and ancient magical traditions.

## House Alephor

The Grand Hierophant leads the most influential noble house:

| **Specialty** | Magical combat |

## VOL'ANLEK

Under Alephor's leadership, the high elven kingdom maintains:

- **Population:** 140,000 high elves
- **Capital:** Great city built upon ancient "Forebearer" jungle ruins
- **Territory:** Control of the northwest realms
- **Legacy:** Oldest race to inhabit the continent

## Philosophy

Ludus Alephor is characterized by:

- **Ruthlessness** - Willing to do whatever necessary for elven supremacy
- **Dogmatism** - Strict adherence to ancient traditions
- **Mistrust of outsiders** - Slow to change, suspicious of other races
- **Magical mastery** - One of the most powerful wizards alive

## Relationship with Striker Horun

The two most powerful elves share an uneasy alliance:
- Alephor controls religion, politics, and magic
- Horun controls the military
- Together they rule VOL'ANLEK, but tensions exist
