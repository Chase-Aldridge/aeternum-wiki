# GRAND HIEROPHANT LUDUS ALEPHOR

**Race:** High Elf
**Class:** Archmage
**Known Equipment:** Staff of the Magi
**Headquarters:** Alephor Palace
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
**Power Tier:** S

## Background

Grand Hierophant Ludus Alephor is a ruthless and dogmatic archmage, the current political and spiritual leader of the kingdom of VOL'ANLEK.

He leads GREAT HOUSE ALEPHOR, the oldest and most religious house of the high elves, masters of magical combat using wizards, sorcerers, clerics, and druids.
