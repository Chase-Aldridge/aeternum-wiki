## Background

King Huren Dragonhelm leads CLAN DRAGONHELM, the oldest and most influential clan of dwarven elders and seers. An old war ally of Emperor Aldrick Ironshield, he commands respect through wisdom rather than martial prowess.

## Rune Magic

CLAN DRAGONHELM's legendary abilities:
- **Influence politics** through magical divination
- **Predict omens** from their god Grungnar
- **Control "The Shadowforge"** - A place of great power
- **Runecraft** that is unmatched in all Gul'Rath

## Alliance with Ironshield

King Huren and Emperor Aldrick:
- Old war allies from THE RED CRUSADE
- Form the backbone of dwarven unity
- Combine military might with spiritual guidance
