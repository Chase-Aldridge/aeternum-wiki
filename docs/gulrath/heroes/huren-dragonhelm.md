# KING HUREN DRAGONHELM

**Race:** Hill Dwarf
**Class:** Rune Priest
**Known Equipment:** Hammer of Grungnar
**Headquarters:** Castle Dragonhelm
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Power Tier:** B

## Background

King Huren Dragonhelm leads CLAN DRAGONHELM, the oldest and most influential clan of dwarven elders and seers. An old war ally of Emperor Ironshield.

Their runecraft and magic is legendary - they currently control "The Shadowforge" and use rune magic to influence politics and predict omens from their god.
