# COMMANDER STRIKER HORUN

**Race:** High Elf
**Class:** General
**Known Equipment:** Blade of Helion
**Headquarters:** The Golden Citadel
**Alignment:** Lawful Good
**Power Tier:** S

## Background

Commander Striker Horun is "elvenkind's strongest soldier" and general of the Golden Legions. He defeated the betrayer Archknight Maldrake and his wild elf bloodletters to end The Red Crusade.

He leads THE GOLDEN LEGION, the remnants of the great host summoned to battle in THE RED CRUSADE, ruled over by the lesser noble HOUSE HORUN.
