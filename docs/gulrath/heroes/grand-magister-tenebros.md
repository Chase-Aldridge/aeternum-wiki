# GRAND MAGISTER TENEBROS

**Race:** Tiefling
**Class:** Arch Magister
**Known Equipment:** Cloak of Shadows
**Headquarters:** Cormandir
**Alignment:** Lawful Evil
**Power Tier:** S+

## Background

Grand Magister Tenebros leads THE MAGISTERIUM and rules CORMANDIR, the only non-vassalized power remaining after THE RED CRUSADE.

He is rumored to have conducted diabolical experiments to contact The Nine Hells and The Outer Realm. Their magic spells and rituals come at excruciating prices when offered.
