# LORD ZAZ CRIMSONSCALE

**Race:** Red Yuan-Ti
**Class:** Duke
**Known Equipment:** Scepter of Tiamat
**Headquarters:** The Trimarken
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
**Power Tier:** A

## Background

Lord Zaz Crimsonscale leads THE TRINITUM, the oldest order among the yuan-ti, ruled over by lines of red nobility for generations.

Religious and curious to obtain power in the shadows, using warlocks, bards, barbarians, clerics, and wizards. Currently seeking to spread their teachings and influence abroad.
