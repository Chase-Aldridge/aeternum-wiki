# QUEEN RERUMAJ CLOUDFEATHER

**Race:** Aarakocra
**Class:** Seer Sorcerer
**Known Equipment:** Dreamwand
**Headquarters:** Skyfall
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral
**Power Tier:** B

## Background

Queen Rerumaj Cloudfeather rules DRAGMAR AER and leads THE INFINITE AERIE, a coalition of merchant lords, master traders, and winged plutocrats.

Their alliance with fire and earth djinn has made their military forces masters of fast attack tactics using assassins, rogues, wizards, knights, and artificers.
