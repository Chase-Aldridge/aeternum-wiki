# SEAMASTER LAEDAERE

**Race:** Sea Elf
**Class:** Grand Admiral
**Known Equipment:** The Pearl Trident
**Headquarters:** Ithiltara Port
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Power Tier:** B

## Background

Seamaster Laedaere leads ITHILTARA and THE SONS OF THE SEA, a powerful alliance of fleet admirals, merchant vessels, and wealthy warlords of the oceans.

One of the best sources of information about the high seas and trade, currently seeking to consolidate trade power and create new sea maps beyond the current continent.
