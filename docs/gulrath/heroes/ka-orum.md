# WARCHIEF KA ORUM

**Race:** Half Orc
**Class:** Berserker
**Known Equipment:** Mountain Claw
**Headquarters:** Jalurian
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Power Tier:** C

## Background

Warchief Ka Orum is a benevolent half-orc who wishes to see his surviving people and the human exiles co-exist.

He leads THE EXILES, a spread out people from all kingdoms seeking asylum due to crime, wanderlust, religious practice, and political exile. Loosely centered around JALURIAN.
