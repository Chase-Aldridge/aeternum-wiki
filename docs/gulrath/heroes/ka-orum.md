## Background

Warchief Ka Orum is a benevolent half-orc leader who wishes to see his surviving people and human exiles co-exist in peace. His people carry the burden of their orc ancestors' near-extermination by the dwarves.

## A Haven for Outcasts

JALURIAN welcomes:
- Pirates, explorers, freebooters, sailors
- Human exiles seeking new homes
- Treasure hunters and archaeologists
- Those who don't fit elsewhere

## The Orc Legacy

Ka Orum's people bear a heavy history:
- Orc ancestors nearly exterminated by dwarves
- Half-orc clans seek peace, not war
- Accepted human exiles who share their outcast status
- Work to build something new

## Dreams of Peace

Ka Orum hopes for:
- Peaceful coexistence between races
- Recognition from the great powers
- A future where his people aren't judged by ancestry
- The Explorer's Association as a path forward
