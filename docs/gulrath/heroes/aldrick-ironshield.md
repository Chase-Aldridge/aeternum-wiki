# EMPEROR ALDRICK IRONSHIELD

**Race:** Mountain Dwarf
**Class:** Warmaster
**Known Equipment:** Shield of the Ancients
**Headquarters:** Ironhelm Keep
**Alignment:** Lawful Good
**Power Tier:** S

## Background

Emperor Aldrick Ironshield is from the most influential dwarven clan with the best smiths and warriors. He was chosen in a landslide among the dwarven elders.

He leads CLAN IRONSHIELD, the most powerful dwarven clan of peerless warriors and smiths. His benevolent rule has brought great peace to his people.
