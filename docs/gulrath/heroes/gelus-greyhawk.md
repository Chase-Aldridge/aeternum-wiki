# LORD GELUS GREYHAWK

**Race:** Gnome
**Class:** Politician
**Known Equipment:** Seal of Xan Yae
**Headquarters:** Saint Helensberg
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Power Tier:** C

## Background

Lord Gelus Greyhawk is the recently elected gnome noble of SAINT HELENSBERG, leading THE FREE PEOPLES.

He wishes to see religious peace and wealthy piety return to the realm. The city is one of the most religious places in the lands, where pilgrims and acolytes come often.
