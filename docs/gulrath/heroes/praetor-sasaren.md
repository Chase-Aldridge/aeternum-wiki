## Background

Praetor Sasaren is the de facto military dictator of TAMARYEN, the largest city on the continent of Gul'Rath. As a Black Yuan-Ti, he rose from the warrior class to seize absolute power through THE BLACK ORDER.

He is known throughout the realm for his **cruelty towards prisoners of war**.

### Yuan-Ti Society
- **Red Yuan-Ti** are considered nobility
- **Black Yuan-Ti** are the warrior class
- Notorious for military juntas and rigorous laws
- Trade in slaves (humans most prized) and intelligence

## Military Power

TAMARYEN possesses:
- Largest numerical standing military
- Largest naval fleets
- Control of the northern seas
- Total power over kingdom's government

## Rivalry with The Trinitum

Sasaren's BLACK ORDER competes with THE TRINITUM:
- Trinitum represents Red Yuan-Ti nobility
- Led by Lord Zaz Crimsonscale
- Religious vs Military factions vie for power
