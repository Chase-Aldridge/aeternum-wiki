# PRAETOR SASAREN

**Race:** Black Yuan-Ti
**Class:** General
**Known Equipment:** Helm of the Serpent God
**Headquarters:** Castle Zaranhar
**Alignment:** Neutral Evil
**Power Tier:** A

## Background

Praetor Sasaren is the current de facto military dictator of TAMARYEN, known for his cruelty towards prisoners of war.

He leads THE BLACK ORDER, a newly risen faction of warrior class black yuan-ti who serve him without question. Cruel masters of war and espionage, notorious for its rogues, necromancers, rangers, and fighters.
