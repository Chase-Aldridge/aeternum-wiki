# LOR - The World

**LOR** is the planet and world where all campaigns take place. Tales and myths have been told about the formation of the world by the gods since the birth of the younger races.

## Physical Properties

- **Size**: Approximately 130% larger than Earth
- **Geography**: Super-continental with vast oceanic masses
- **Climate**: Contains all major biomes and climates known
- **Poles**: Interestingly, the extreme poles are subtropical or tundra, while the ice belt is closer to the equator

## Metaphysical Truths

Various dogmas and schools of thought exist regarding timelines and metaphysical truths. However, the reality is that no one truly knows the origins of the world.

The planet has shifted dramatically since the Aeternum Crisis of 1111 AE, as the winds of magic have changed and geopolitics have opened up across all continents.

## The Planes

The multiverse connected to LOR includes:
- **The Material Plane** - Where LOR exists
- **The Feywild** - Realm of fey creatures
- **The Shadowfell** - Realm of shadow and death
- **Mount Celestia** - Home of lawful good deities
- **The Nine Hells** - Realm of devils
- **The Abyss** - Realm of demons
- **Limbo** - Realm of chaos
- And many more...
