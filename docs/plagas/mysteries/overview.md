## The Titanic Beasts

Colossal creatures that roam PLAGAS, drawing hunters from across LOR.

### The Great Hunt
All major colonial powers are participating in a titan hunt:
- **Fort Runestone** - Duergar forces in the north
- **Windrun** - Warforged legions through the center
- **Lozenberg** - Pirate princes and merchant-bandits
- **The Explorer's Association** - Elite agents from Gul'Rath

### Unknown Origins
Where do the titanic beasts come from? Theories include:
- Natural evolution in isolated ecosystems
- Magical corruption or enhancement
- Ancient experiments gone wrong
- Planar influences
- Divine or demonic creation

## The Three Titans of Creation

Scholarly wizards from Gul'Rath discuss the first beings that incarnated in the multiverse and created the planes, the planet Lor, and all other gods:

### Exarian, the Titan of Light
- **Status:** Myth
- First and oldest of the Titans
- Created light and matter itself
- Has never been described or seen
- Believed to sit atop his own plane writing in **THE WORLDTOME** - a book of metareality containing past, present, and future

### Kronos, Titan of Time
- **Status:** Myth
- Second and darkest of the Titans
- Created temporality and mortality
- Worshipped by some factions within THE MAGISTERIUM
- His dark avatar was summoned and banished during the Aeternum Crisis of 1111AE

### Alariel, Titan of Magic
- **Status:** Myth
- Third and youngest of the Titans
- Created the weave and winds of magic
- Helped Exarian create the various pantheons of gods
- Protects them from Kronos's devouring appetite
- Has many forms and names in every culture
