---

## Description

A research and magical colony run by THE MAGISTERIUM of GUL'RATH. Little is known about the tower other than their steady streams of supply ships and expeditions into the jungle visible from outside their walls.

---

## Operations

### Research
Highly secretive and reclusive mages, warlocks, wizards, and sorcerers conduct experimental spellcraft within the tower's walls.

### Expeditions
Regular magical expeditions venture into the jungle for purposes unknown to outsiders.

### Secrecy
The Sapphire Tower maintains extreme operational security - few who enter ever speak of what they see inside.

---

## Leadership

### High Magister Telerian

The archmage who leads the Sapphire Tower colony. Rumored to be supporting Fort Runestone armies on behalf of The Magisterium through:
- Clandestine scrying operations
- Secret teleportation circles
- Mercenary battlemages
- Potent magical artifacts

---

## The Titan Hunt

The Sapphire Tower's role in the titan hunt is believed to be primarily support and intelligence rather than direct military action. Their magical capabilities make them invaluable to allied forces.

---

## Mysteries

What exactly the mages are researching in PLAGAS remains unknown. Theories include:
- Ancient magical artifacts
- Planar convergence points
- The source of the titanic beasts
- Lost Forebearer knowledge
- The nature of the winds of magic

---
