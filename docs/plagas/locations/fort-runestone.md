## Description

A well-established Duergar mining and slaving colony from CTHON. The dark dwarves have come to PLAGAS to:
- Pillage the rich natural ore veins
- Capture valuable Leonin and Loxodon slaves
- Gather rare plant and monster raw materials

---

## History

Fort Runestone is the **oldest colony known** on PLAGAS, predating all other foreign settlements. Despite their age and power, the duergar are hesitant to welcome outsiders.

---

## Operations

### Mining
The primary purpose of the colony is extraction of valuable ores from the mineral-rich lands of PLAGAS.

### Slaving
Indigenous Leonin and Loxodon are captured and sold into slavery, making Fort Runestone one of the most reviled settlements among the native peoples.

### Monster Hunting
They employ ample bounty hunters, monster trappers, and mercenary forces internationally to gather rare materials from the dangerous beasts of PLAGAS.

---

## Leadership

### General Korax

The military commander leading duergar and drow mercenary forces in the north camp to hunt the titanic beasts. Known for:
- Ruthless efficiency
- Military discipline
- Willingness to employ any means necessary

---

## The Titan Hunt

Fort Runestone is actively participating in the great hunt for the titanic beasts of PLAGAS, sending well-equipped expeditions into the dangerous interior.

---

## Relations

### Allies
- **The Sapphire Tower** - The Magisterium secretly supports Fort Runestone with clandestine scrying, teleportation circles, mercenary battlemages, and potent artifacts

### Enemies
- **The Red Mane Tribe** - Leonin warbands ambush and scalp any Fort Runestone forces on sight
- **The White Fang Tribe** - Hunters contest the duergar incursions
- **All Indigenous Peoples** - The slave trade has made them universally hated
