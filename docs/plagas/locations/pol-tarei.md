## Description

A free city of unknown founding deep in the southern islands, largely run by sailors, pirates, hunters, and mercenaries from across LOR.

Here some freed leonin and loxodon slaves, as well as those claiming to be from the jungle tribes, have begun to integrate with denizens from other lands.

---

## Purpose

The city largely organizes:
- Merchant fleets
- Jungle expeditions for profit
- Monster hunting parties
- Trade missions

Has even done recent business with THE EXPLORER'S ASSOCIATION.

---

## Leadership

### Markus Swiftsail

The leader of Pol'Tarei, commanding:
- His flagship "EMPIRE"
- A small fleet of buccaneers
- The city's mercenary forces

Currently supporting the Lozenberg expedition in the titan hunt.

---

## Key Locations

### The Sea Drake
The main tavern on Pol Tarei, run by **Tyr**, an old elf barkeep. A hub for:
- Information gathering
- Job postings
- Mercenary recruitment
- Rumors and legends

---

## Notable Figures

### Grey Skar
The oldest Leonin freed slave in Pol Tarei. Former **White Fang** council member who had a failed hunt and was ousted. A source of knowledge about the indigenous peoples.

### The Lark
An Elven female Bard who plays "The Ballad of the Rusitauri" - the legendary tale of the heroes from the Aeternum Crisis.

### The Octopus
A drow criminal lord who frequents "The Lusty Mermaid" brothel each evening. Controls much of the black market through his lieutenant **Ranos**, a half-elf.

## Campaign Starting Point

Pol Tarei serves as the entry point for new heroes arriving in PLAGAS. Its relatively welcoming nature and diverse population make it ideal for adventurers seeking their fortune in the wild continent.
