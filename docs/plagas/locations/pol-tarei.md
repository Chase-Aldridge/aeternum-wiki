## Description

A free city of unknown founding deep in the southern islands, largely run by:
- Sailors and pirates
- Hunters and mercenaries
- Travelers from across LOR

Some freed leonin and loxodon slaves have begun to integrate with denizens from other lands, along with those claiming to be from jungle tribes.

The city organizes **merchant fleets** and **jungle expeditions** for profit, recently doing business with The Explorer's Association.
