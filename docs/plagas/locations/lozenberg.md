# LOZENBERG

*Free City of Outlaws and Traders*

---

## Description

A small free city colony originally founded by outlaws, traders, sailors, and hunters migrating across the seas from GUL'RATH.

This settlement is home to a myriad of races, religions, and classes from across LOR, seeking fortune or exile on the wilderness frontier.

---

## Character

The city is perhaps the **most welcoming to travelers**, though your coin purse is as likely to be cut as your throat should trouble arise in this lawless colony.

### What You'll Find
- Adventurers seeking fortune
- Criminals in exile
- Merchants of questionable goods
- Hunters and trappers
- Pirates and smugglers
- Those with nowhere else to go

---

## Leadership

### The Sand Sparrow

The enigmatic leader of Lozenberg who commands:
- Merchant-bandits
- Pirate princes
- The chaotic populace

Currently mobilizing forces in the titan hunt at the behest of the Gul'Rath nobles who helped found the colony.

---

## The Titan Hunt

Lozenberg has formed an expedition force to participate in the great hunt, combining:
- Experienced hunters and trappers
- Pirate ships and crews
- Mercenaries and adventurers
- Support from The Explorer's Association

---

## The Explorer's Association

### Darius Kane
Former Chief of The Explorer's Association, rumored to have traveled to Plagas personally with elite agents to ensure that Gul'Rath forces win the hunt.

### Enzo
Lord Commander of the Plagas Expedition - Explorer's Association

---

## Economy

Lozenberg thrives on:
- Trade in exotic goods from the jungle
- Smuggling operations
- Bounty hunting services
- Mercenary contracts
- Information brokering
