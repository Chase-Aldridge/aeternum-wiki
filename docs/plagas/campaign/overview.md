## Act I: Paydirt

### Chapter I - "A Savage Land" (1116.1AE, Level 1)

The heroes arrive in PLAGAS as strangers in a wild land.

**Key Events:**
- **Ragur Thorne** - Bounty hunter from Xonos, hires the party to hunt
- **Bron** (halfling warrior) and **Esra** (human rogue) join as mercenaries
- The party visits **The Sea Drake**, the main tavern on Pol Tarei
- **The Lark** - An elven bard plays the "Ballad of the Rusitauri"
- Target: A halfling fugitive from Zonos wanted for murder, theft, and larceny
- **Grey Skar** - Oldest Leonin freed slave, formerly of White Fang tribe
- Each hero paid 7GP/day

**Top Narcotics in Plagas:**
| Drug | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Tomb Honey | Unknown |
| Jungle Grass | 1hr: +1 WIS, -1 INT |
| Sweet Wheat | 1hr: +1 CON, -1 CHA |
| Toad Rot | Unknown |

### Chapter II - "A Savage Land" (1116.1AE, Level 1)

**Key Events:**
- Meet **Myrra**, an overly cheery female drow at the docks
- Meet **Gurn**, a black-skinned tiefling offering dice games
- Learn about **"The Octopus"**, a drow criminal lord at "The Lusty Mermaid" brothel
- **Jorin the Rat** (halfling mark) works for the Octopus, as does Ragur
- The Octopus's lieutenant: **Ranos** (half elf)
- Rumor: Fort Runestone recently hit an island west of Pol Tarei, slaving Leonin
- **Keel** trades his heirloom to meet the Leonin community
- **Tyr** - Old elf barkeep of the Sea Drake
- **Markus Swiftsail** - His HQ is massive galleon "The Sea Empire"
- **Prophlos** - Ancient loxodon shaman
- Party ambushed by 3 aarakocra thieves from **The Shadow Hand** (legendary thieves guild from Zonos)

**Local Terms:**
- **"Maktin"** - One who is an outside insider in Leonin culture
- **Yondelberries** - Choice red tasty berry native to Plagas

### Chapter III - "A Savage Land" (1116.2AE, Level 2)

*1 month training montage*

**Key Events:**
- 90GP of goods secured from Markus for the Leonin village
- Investigated brigands west of town
- Children found explosive poison gas artifact - 2 killed
- Tasked with finding and returning the artifact
- Potion merchants from The Sapphire Tower making wild claims
- Keel drinks blue Baja blast vial - can now tell time/date accurately (addicted)
- Artifact sold to the Octopus (believed to be duergar make)
- Heard about "red scales" fish migration - gall bladders sell for 1000GP each
- **Jerry** has magic sea shell (cast gust of wind 1/week)
- Fishing expedition: caught mako shark, red scales, sea snakes
- **The Baron** and his men from Lozenberg attack and take their catch
- Met **Grug** (half orc captain) and **Uglup** (goblin rogue)
