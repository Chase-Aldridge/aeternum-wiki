# Plagas

A massive rainforest and desert archipelago to the far west. This wild frontier is home to leonin tribes, loxodon shamans, colonial settlements, and titanic beasts. Setting of **The Plagas Prophecy** campaign.

## Indigenous Peoples

- [Indigenous Overview](/docs/plagas/indigenous/overview) - Native cultures of Plagas

### The Leonin Tribes
Proud lion-folk who have inhabited Plagas for millennia. They view the colonial settlers with suspicion and guard ancient secrets.

### The Loxodon Shamans
Elephant-folk mystics who commune with the spirits of the land. Keepers of prophecy and ancient lore.

---

## Mysteries of Plagas

- [Mysteries Overview](/docs/plagas/mysteries/overview) - Unexplained phenomena

### The Za'an Ruins
Ancient structures predating all known civilizations. Who built them? What secrets do they hold?

### The Golden City
Legends speak of a city of gold hidden deep in the jungle. Many have sought it. Few have returned.

### The Titans
Colossal beings that slumber beneath the earth. The prophecy speaks of their awakening...
