# CULT OF PELOR

| | |
|---|---|
| **Leader** | Various Chaplains and Clerics |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |

## Description

The fastest growing cult on AETERNUM following the crisis.

PELOR, the neutral good god of the sun and agriculture, has gained a large cult following among the commoners in all kingdoms due to the influx of traders worshiping in his name from GUL'RATH.

Led by chaplain paladins, prophetic clerics, and warm traders traveling far and wide.
