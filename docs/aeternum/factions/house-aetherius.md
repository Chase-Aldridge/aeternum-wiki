# House Aetherius

**The Magical Dominion**

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Power Rating | 8/10 |
| Alignment | Lawful Neutral |
| Leader | High Inquisitor Valus |
| Seat | [Aetherius](/docs/aeternum/locations/aetherius) |
| Specialty | Divination, Augury, Intelligence, Control |

## Overview

House Aetherius is known for its long reign of **military and magical dominance** over the last 200 years of recorded history. Generally seen as the political "leader" among the great houses, the nobles of the Aetherius bloodline are unflinching in their dominance and assertion of the status quo.

**Notable:** They openly serve **no formal god** among the pantheon.

## Military Power

Commands vast legions of:
- Highly trained fighters
- Paladins
- Wizards
- Clerics

Has been able to control commerce and politics through:
- Prowess on the field of battle
- Diplomatic maneuvering

## Key Strongholds

| Location | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [Aetherius](/docs/aeternum/locations/aetherius) | Imperial capital, Obsidian Citadel |
| Tower of Dusk | Northern fortress, magical arsenal, powerful scrying stone |
| Tower of Dawn | Southern citadel, military training (LARGELY DESTROYED) |

### The Tower of Dusk
- Population: 30,000
- Most powerful magic users trained and stationed here
- Rumored to contain a potent **scrying stone** explaining omniscient abilities
- The Angel of Knowledge was imprisoned here
- Now frontline bulwark against the Red Horde

### The Tower of Dawn
- Population: 50,000 (former)
- High generals and legion warmasters stationed here
- New recruits armed, tested, indoctrinated
- **DESTROYED** during Outer Realm portal opening during blood moon
- Land now filled with eldritch magic and psionic distortions
- Created mutants known as **The Forsaken**

## Political Relations

Recently solidified dominance through a **pact with traditional enemy House Dargo** - shocking the other houses. They publicly pledge to bring peace after crushing the Red Horde.

## The Inquisition

A secretive organization reporting only to High Inquisitor Valus:
- Operates in shadows of traditional politics
- Intention: Purge all "unworthy" magical beings
- House Nejune rightly fears them
- Leader: [Lord Garrulien](/docs/aeternum/characters/garrulien)

## Leadership

### High Inquisitor Valus
- **Tier:** ???
- Not seen in public for over 100 years
- Rumored to be dead or bargained for magically extended life
- Allegedly wielded the **Scepter of Aether** (commune with planes)

### High Commander Taris
- **Tier:** S+
- Ward and steward, handles public dealings
- Known for brutal willpower and lack of mercy
- Executes all he conquers in battle
- Wields the legendary **Spear of Longinem**
- Genius political schemer, not war strategist

### Lord Garrulien
- **Tier:** S
- Leader of The Inquisition
- Shrewd and subtle demeanor
- Possesses **Sarasin's Ring** (nullify/absorb magical essence)
- Stationed at Tower of Dusk

## Current Status (1116 AE)

- Aetherius is now demilitarized, ruled by joint council
- Political power diminished but magical influence remains
- Alliance with Dargo collapsed after the Crisis
- Tower of Dawn lands remain corrupted
