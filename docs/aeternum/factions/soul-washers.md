# THE SOUL WASHERS

| | |
|---|---|
| **Leader** | Kishimoto Draconblade |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |

## Description

An order of dragonborn warriors who follow the way of Dragon Bushido.

KISHIMOTO DRACONBLADE became the new master with BENGROR THE MIGHTY as his right hand sword. YURIO ZARASHI aids in training as the third sword of the village.

The DRACONISS dragonborn founded their own island village named WAJA-SAN in the EMERALD ISLES to honor their sensei and his peaceful death in 1112AE.
