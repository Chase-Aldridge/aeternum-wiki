## Overview

The up-and-coming heroes and protagonists who met under fated auspices at **The Laughing Dragon** in Meria on **1111.1 AE** and decided to adventure together.

They completed quests and garnered favor with House Meria and the Fellwood Watch before becoming the legendary figures who faced The Aeternum Crisis.

## Allies: The Silver Sentinels

A band of heroes from Dargo seeking glory and honor:

- **Tier:** S
- Human Monk, leader
- Made **Heavenly Pact** with Lokhir (superhuman abilities, cost half lifespan)
- Devastated by losses, traveled solo toward Nejune for vengeance
- Now training next generation at The Conin dojo in Dargo

### Amaud Sturnstar
- **Tier:** B (DECEASED)
- Human Wizard, House Sturnstar noble
- **CAPTURED AND SLAIN** by Yon'Tal with Dagger of Heronimus
- Soul banished to darkness, requires Wish to revive
- Later recovered as "broken" version

### Elena Dragonsong
- **Tier:** B (MIA)
- Human Bard, former court bard
- Devastated by Amaud's death
- Summoned demon via blood pact
- Disappeared with demon to exact revenge on Yon'Tal

### Christof Caladen
- **Tier:** A
- Human Master Thief
- Fast-talking "glue" of the group
- Wields red **Drakwald Daggers**
- **Post-Crisis:** Became one of richest individuals with The Collector, feared duo reputation

## Key Accomplishments

1. Freed Ferin Greenreed from goblin captivity
2. Cleansed Tomb of Saint Bel'Raa
3. Infiltrated House Nejune
4. Slew Lord Neerius (Ancient Vampire) in the Catacombs
5. Raided Yon'Tal's manor, freed slaves
6. Discovered and exposed traitors in Dargo
7. Faced The Aeternum Crisis and saved the continent
8. Assisted The Voice in brokering new geopolitical peace

## Legacy (1116 AE)

- Seen as legendary protectors by many
- Viewed as political-extremist vigilantes by others
- Helped stabilize Meria, Dargo, and Thane's Hold
- Established HQ in Guild tower (Meria) and Shadow Citadel (Limbo)
- Songs and tales of "The Rusitauri" sung across the land
