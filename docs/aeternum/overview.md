# Aeternum

**AETERNUM** is an isolated major continent in the world of LOR where the original campaign began. This land of political intrigue, ancient magic, and epic battles served as the backdrop for "The Aeternum Crisis" of 1111 AE.

## Geography

Aeternum features diverse terrain including:
- Central lowlands dominated by Aetherius
- Dense forests of the Fellwood
- The Gray Mountains and Wastes to the north
- Coastal regions controlled by Dargo and Meria
- The mysterious Shadowlands of Nejune

## Major Cities

- **Aetherius** (pop. 900,000) - Imperial seat and center of power
- **Meria** (pop. 1,300,000) - Largest city, cosmopolitan free port
- **Dargo** (pop. 440,000) - Naval stronghold and religious center
- **Nejune** (pop. 210,000) - City of necromancers (DESTROYED in 1111 AE)
- **Thane's Hold** (pop. 100,000) - Dwarven fortress (Upper holds FALLEN)

## Timeline of Major Events

### Pre-Crisis
- **~300 AE** - Clan Thorgrem at height of power
- **~1000 AE** - Ugluk the Red begins uniting the Red Horde

### The Aeternum Crisis (1111 AE)
- **1111.1 AE** - The Fellowship meets at The Laughing Dragon in Meria
- **1111.2 AE** - Lord Neerius released from the Second Seal
- **1111.8 AE** - Events at Magus Rise

### Post-Crisis (1112+ AE)
- Nejune destroyed by "The Black Tyrant" tarrasque
- The Guild rises to control Aeternum from the shadows
- Magical mist surrounding the continent falls
- Contact established with other continents

## Current State (1116 AE)

Following the Crisis:
- **Aetherius** is now a demilitarized trade zone ruled by joint council
- **Meria** is the strongest power, led by Lady Ellyssa Rorinstar and Lady Nynora
- **Dargo** is ruled by the Cult of Dragonspear under "Ryland the Pious"
- **Thane's Hold** has been slowly retaken from the Red Horde
- **Nejune** lies in cursed ruins, surrounded by violet miasma
- **The Fellwood** is controlled by the mysterious "Viridian Wolf"
