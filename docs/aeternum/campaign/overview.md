# The Aeternum Crisis

*The complete chronicle of the Fellowship of the Blade*

## Campaign Timeline

The events take place in the year 1111AE on the continent of AETERNUM.

## Acts

### [Act I: The Call](/docs/aeternum/campaign/act-1)
The fellowship forms and embarks on their first adventures. Levels 1-6.

### [Act II: Heroic Awakening](/docs/aeternum/campaign/act-2)
The heroes grow in power and face greater threats. Levels 7-10.

### [Act III: Turning of the Tide](/docs/aeternum/campaign/act-3)
War engulfs the continent as the end times approach. Levels 11-14.

### [Act IV: The End Times Prophecy](/docs/aeternum/campaign/act-4)
The final battle against Kronus and the fate of Aeternum. Levels 15-19.
