# Act II: Heroic Awakening

*Chapters 7-15 | Levels 7-10 | 1111.2AE - 1111.3AE*

The fellowship grows in power and renown, but darkness tests their bonds.

---

## Chapter VII: The Council of the Wood
**1111.2AE | Level 7**

The heroes attended the great COUNCIL OF THE WOOD at FELLWOOD WATCH. Present were:
- **MISTRAYA** - Who opened with a blessing to ALAYA
- **AENARION & FAYRUS** - Urging unity against the RED HORDE
- **LIONEL BLACKPEBBLE** - Pleading for THANE'S HOLD
- **GROMWINSTON** - Representing MERIA
- **LORD TARTULIEN** - For HOUSE AETHERIUS (booed for abandoning the Gray Gate)
- **LADY MERA** - For DARGO
- **Hooded NEJUNE mages** - Denying responsibility for the Blight

ZADSHEN rose and gave a rousing speech proclaiming the need for unity against evil.

After the council, MISTRAYA and AENARION revealed to the heroes:
- The mist surrounding Aeternum is unnatural
- UGLUK THE RED may have become the Avatar of Bel'Mog
- THE INQUISITION destroyed all records of pre-mist times
- They fear VISSIGERON THE CRUEL, the ancient green dragon

The heroes were tasked with infiltrating NEJUNE to end the Blight before the Blood Moon.

---

## Chapter VIII: The Tomb of Bel'Raa
**1111.2AE | Level 8**

Armed with magical equipment, the party ventured to the TOMB OF SAINT BEL'RAA:
- Found the entrance desecrated with black eldritch runes
- Battled ghosts, skeletal warriors, and a shadow fiend
- Discovered ULRICK's fey pact was affecting his magic
- Faced MALDRAK THE BLACKENED - a demilich!

In a titanic battle:
- SHO was cursed
- ULRICK was laid low
- ZADSHEN became paralyzed
- ARCA nearly died heroically

They destroyed Maldrak's physical form and recovered SAINT BEL'RAA's artifacts, including the **Amulet of Antimagic**.

---

## Chapter IX: Darkness Tests the Fellowship
**1111.2AE | Level 9**

The fellowship journeyed through the SHADOWLANDS, but tensions rose:

- **The Demon of Rage** manifested from SHO's sword and attacked the party
- **ULRICK** failed to contact OBERON and grew desperate
- **LORD IRELLION**, a servant of VISSIGERON, appeared offering dark bargains
- The heroes refused the fey lord's temptations

They approached the TOMB OF SAINT BEL'RAA to end the source of the Blight, with only weeks until the Blood Moon.

---

## Chapter X: Ulrick Falls
**Last days of 1111.2AE | Level 10**

ULRICK's darkness consumed him:
- **OBERON** commanded him to eliminate NEBULOS, a noble in NEJUNE
- In the MAUSOLEUMS OF RUIN, they encountered KARNAC SHADOWSPELL's spirit
- ULRICK attacked an AETHERIUS caravan with wild magic
- He aged 18 years and his hair turned stark white

### The Death

In battle against AETHERIUS soldiers:
- ULRICK fell unconscious from spear wounds
- A soldier drove a spear through his skull, killing him
- ARCA called upon ALAYA's divine intervention
- **ULRICK was resurrected**, but physically weakened forever

The fellowship confronted him about his descent into darkness...

---

## Chapter XI: To Nejune
**1111.3AE | Level 10**

The party infiltrated NEJUNE:
- Slayed guards who refused to let them pass
- Freed gnome slaves "Boy 1" and "Boy 2"
- Learned THANE'S HOLD had fallen to THE RED HORDE
- Discovered bounties of 1,000GP on each of their heads

---

## Chapter XII: The Catacombs
**1111.3AE | Level 10**

Descending into the NEJUNE CATACOMBS:
- Navigated trapped corridors and ancient tapestries
- ZADSHEN polymorphed into a black dragon (from a cursed potion!)
- Found a demon general bound in arcane shackles
- Battled Umber Hulks in the darkness

---

## Chapter XIII: Zadshen's Sacrifice
**1111.3AE | Level 10**

Deep in the Catacombs, the heroes encountered:
- THE NEJUNE THIEVES GUILD demanding they turn back
- Bounties distributed among all factions
- Multiple enemy parties converging on their position

### The Battle with LORD NEERIUS

The ancient vampire, released from the second sealed gate, appeared:
- Instantly killed mages with claws and curses
- SHO transformed into a fiery demon form
- ZADSHEN charged the vampire with devastating power smites
- NEERIUS summoned vargheists who tore out ZADSHEN's throat

**ARCA revived ZADSHEN from death**, scarring his neck forever.

In a final heroic moment:
- ULRICK dropped a sunburst, shrinking himself two feet
- SHO won his inner battle with the demon
- SHO dealt the killing blow, destroying NEERIUS's soul

THE SILVER SENTINELS arrived to witness "absolute cinema."

---

## Divine Revelations

After the battle, each hero received visions:

**SHOREA** - BAHAMUT revealed:
- The Demon of Rage shares a twin soul with the Angel of Will
- Three such pairings exist across Aeternum
- One in the TOWER OF DUSK, one in a MERIA galleon

**ZADSHEN** - LEVIATHAN showed:
- Ancient alliance between Celestials and HOUSE AETHERIUS
- Vision of a celestial kingdom or burning destruction
- The Qunari order tied to his bloodline

**ULRICK** - SILVANUS showed:
- A timeline where Feywild and Fellwood unite
- A timeline where the forests burn

**ARCA** - ALAYA warned:
- There may come a time where he lays down his life for her
