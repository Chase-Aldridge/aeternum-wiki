# Act IV: The End Times Prophecy

*Chapters 23-29 | Levels 15-19 | 1111.4AE - 1112AE*

The final battle for Aeternum. Gods fall, heroes rise, and the world is forever changed.

---

## The Prophecy Fulfilled

The Blood Moon of 1111.4AE arrived. Three magical powers harnessed the wild winds:
- MISTRAYA sought to become the Avatar of ALAYA
- UGLUK THE RED had already become the Avatar of BEL'MOG
- HOUSE AETHERIUS pursued knowledge beyond mortal ken

The fate of Aeternum hung in the balance.

---

## Chapter XXIV: The Battle of the Scrolls

ULRICK grabbed the **Scroll of Wish** and made a desperate choice:
- Wished for his companions to return to full power
- They rose from the ground renewed with strength
- Struck down EDORA, who willingly died whispering *"Hail Tiamat"*
- ULRICK felt his ability to ever cast Wish again may be gone

---

## Chapter XXV: Confronting the Dragons

The heroes faced dragons empowered by TIAMAT:
- ULRICK used euphoria breath of the fey to drive one dragon mad
- Cast modify memory to make it believe SHO was a friend
- Turned enemies into allies through cunning magic

---

## Chapter XXVI: The Berserkers Rise

SHO blew the **Horn of Valhalla** and summoned a huge mob of berserkers:
- Warriors engaged alongside the party
- Battled side by side, cutting down foes
- ZAD and SHO led killing sprees through enemy ranks

ULRICK launched magical ordinance but suffered wild magic:
- Skin turned rotting and undead-like
- Turned blue
- Exploded with plague pus from his mouth

Still, he learned new secrets of the arcane.

---

## Chapter XXVII: Leviathan's Truth

ZADSHEN prayed to LEVIATHAN and learned:
- LEVIATHAN is an ally to THE QUNARI
- Though he altered fate with the King Wish
- Though he apparently broke an unknown pact with the celestials
- His mission to the dragon god remained the same

---

## Chapter XXVIII: The Ultimate Confrontation

The fellowship faced the GOLDEN ORDER monk in desperate combat:
- ULRICK and ZAD battled trying to heal SHO
- ZAD managed to land several devastating smites
- ULRICK transformed himself into a terrifying ArchFey

EDORA willingly died on SHO's blade, revealing her allegiance to TIAMAT with her final words.

---

## Chapter XXIX: Resolution

After the fall of NEJUNE and the defeat of the old demigods:

### THE VOICE Ascends
The newest demigod rose to power, taking the Shadow Citadel in LIMBO as their throne - formerly the slain god KET's headquarters.

### The Fellowship's Reward
The heroes were granted:
- Political and financial rewards from THE GUILD
- Staff, followers, equipment, and fleet established in MERIA
- Access to THE SHADOW CITADEL in LIMBO
- Leave to pursue personal quests

### Post-Crisis Fates

**ODA** - Returned to DARGO and the temple of THE CONIN with LUAN BO to train a new generation and develop his own fighting style.

**ZADSHEN** - Was rescued from 17 years of planar torture and retired to MOUNT CELESTIA as an acolyte.

**SHO** - Returned to REGELUM as an avenger, beginning a rebellion against THE RED SHOGUN.

**EGGBERT** - Sacrificed his soul to free ZADSHEN from PANDEMONIUM.

**ULRICK** - His soul escaped to BYTOPIA when OBERON's power broke, now sealed in an adamantine jar.
