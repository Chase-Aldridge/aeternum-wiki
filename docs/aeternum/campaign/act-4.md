# Act IV: The End Times Prophecy

*Levels 15-19 | The Final Battle*

## Summary

The final act brings the epic confrontation with Kronus, the Titan of Time. The heroes enter the Temple of Bahamut, fight the Battle of Dusk, and witness the Red Horde broken as Kronus is reborn.

The Rusitauri journey to Hell itself in a desperate bid to save their fallen comrade. At the Council, they must choose between Exodus or a Last Stand. Finally, they assault Magus Rise floor by floor, confronting the ultimate evil in the depths below.
