# Act III: Turning of the Tide

*Levels 11-14 | War Engulfs the Land*

## Summary

War consumes Aeternum as the End Times prophecy begins to unfold. The Blood Moon Jagai brings cosmic change, and chaos erupts in Dargo.

The heroes must make desperate stands as the Watch falls and Meria itself is lost to darkness. They seek the Isle of the Dragon, hoping for divine intervention as the world crumbles around them.
