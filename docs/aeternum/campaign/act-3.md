# Act III: Turning of the Tide

*Chapters 16-22 | Levels 11-14 | 1111.3AE - 1111.4AE*

War engulfs Aeternum as the End Times prophecy unfolds.

---

## The Blood Moon Approaches

With the Blood Moon of 1111.4AE fast approaching, the fellowship raced against time. The three great magical powers of AETERNUM would harness the wild winds of magic that night:
- **NEJUNE** - For destruction
- **AETHERIUS** - For knowledge
- **BLESSED OF ALAYA** - For ascension

---

## Chapter XVI: Alliances and Betrayals

The party allied with THE SILVER SENTINELS:
- **ZHANG LI** - Leader, clapped arms with the heroes
- **CHRISTOF** - Thief with magical whistle of silence
- **ELENA** - Bard who knew ancient prophecies
- **CALADENN** - Introduced them to black market contacts

ELENA shared a mythic song: *"When all the avatars of the gods walk AETERNUM, there will come a day where it will either be destroyed in a cataclysm or will ascend into its own plane..."*

---

## Chapter XVII: To Dargo

The heroes journeyed to DARGO, where SHO pursued his mission to unite the dragonborn:
- Found 5 dragonborn including elder GAEJEN, young reds KI and KAI
- Merchants TALREN and BERAI with blue scales
- Half-orc slave BJORK and shady man JOHN SMITH joined them

SHO offered to take escaped slaves to DRACONISS, providing supplies and gold to those who chose different paths.

---

## Chapter XVIII: The Dragonborn Return

SHO discovered scattered dragonborn groups:
- **THE BLUE FANGS** - Mercenary band of blue dragonborn
- **THE RED TALONS** - Wayward hunters
- **THE LONE DRAKES** - Ronin samurai

He paid them to meet and help his escaped slaves, dreaming of all dragonborn returning to DRACONISS one day.

At the temple, SHO instructed the dragonborn to study his journals and steel their minds for danger.

---

## Chapter XIX: Unlocking Power

### ULRICK's Mission
ULRICK contacted OBERON and gained more powers of fey magic. He was tasked with:
- Brokering alliance between Feywild and Fellwood leaders
- Establishing a permanent portal

### ZADSHEN's Disgrace
At a noble event, ZADSHEN:
- Hurled his blade at an enemy prince, impaling him
- Was brought down by assassins
- The party survived the chaos

---

## Chapter XX: The Fleet Battle

SHO aided the DARGO fleet in capturing three WARDEN ships and rescued the dragonborn prisoners there.

In naval combat:
- ULRICK unleashed globe of invulnerability
- The heroes took shelter before scorching wardens with dawnburst
- Massive battles on deck with well-placed smites
- Dominated enemy spellcasters with psychic abilities

---

## Chapter XXI: Master Waja's Duel

SHO found MASTER WAJA and experienced **Bankai** for the first time through intense dueling.

Later, when a demon possessed SHO:
- ULRICK "locked in" with moonbeam, freeing him
- SHO leaped in a massive "Obi Wan" jump and killed the demon
- The dragonborn sent scouts to tell their people to return to the village

---

## Chapter XXII: The Isle of the Dragon

The fellowship journeyed to BAHAMUT's sacred isle:

### SHO's Awakening
- Entered the soul of his sword
- Learned the true name of the blade: **REN**
- Underwent trials in an ocean of combat
- Unlocked **Shikai**
- Received the blessing of BAHAMUT
- Saw the next steps to clear the temple at THE ISLE OF THE DRAGON

### ZADSHEN's Leap of Faith
- Communed with his blade and learned its will
- Leaped from a volcano in a test of faith
- Learned how to become the champion of LEVIATHAN
- Needed 1000 followers, a cleric, and a paladin to manifest Leviathan

### ULRICK's Fruit
- Ate a fruit sprouted at the calling of OBERON
- Received his blessing
- Learned what he needed to do next

### The King Wish
ZADSHEN grabbed the **Luck Blade** and made "The King Wish":
- Wished for King BEREN to abdicate, making him king of DARGO
- A huge peal of lightning crashed in the background
- Unknown consequences for changing Fate
- BEREN later called him "usurper"

---

## Chapter XXIII: The Temple of Bahamut

SHO stepped to the altar of BAHAMUT and fell into trance:
- Saw BAHAMUT battling TIAMAT, his nemesis
- Combined power with REN to slay the dragon goddess's influence
- Banished the wind of chaos from the temple
- Became BAHAMUT's Champion
- **Attained Bankai**
- The entire temple shook with power as TIAMAT's banners fell

ULRICK discovered artifacts of BAHAMUT hidden under the shrine: his sword, armor, and orb.
