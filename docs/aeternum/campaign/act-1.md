# Act I: The Call

*Levels 1-6 | The Fellowship Forms*

## Summary

The first act follows the formation of the Fellowship of the Blade at The Laughing Dragon tavern in Meria.

A group of unlikely heroes - a dragonborn samurai, a wild human paladin, a scarred human monk, and others - come together and prove themselves through goblin hunts and early adventures. They journey to the Fellwood, encounter the mysterious Aenarion, and face their first true dangers in the dark forests.
