# LU ZHONG "The Tiger"

**Title:** Warlord of the Twilight Reavers
**Race:** Human

## Description

LU ZHONG THE TIGER is one of the new warlords heading MERIA's elite mercenary force known as THE TWILIGHT REAVERS.

He set sail north in 1114AE with GUILDMASTER OREON, GROMWINSTON, and RYUZEN into uncharted seas.
