# NERO VALUS "The Immortal"

**Title:** The Immortal
**Race:** Unknown

## Description

NERO VALUS "The Immortal" is a legendary figure who participated in the titanic duel at MAGUS RISE.

Along with BLACK FANG and THE PANTOKRATOR, they engaged in a massive magical & psionic battle at the end of 1111AE. Their fates are unknown.
