# ZADSHEN DRAGONSPEAR

*Aasimar Paladin, Champion of Leviathan*

## Overview

**Full Name:** Zadshen Dragonspear
**Race:** Aasimar
**Class:** Paladin (Oath of Vengeance)
**Affiliation:** The Fellowship of the Blade
**Divine Patron:** Leviathan
**Status:** Retired to Mount Celestia

## Background

ZADSHEN was born under mysterious circumstances in DARGO to his late surrogate father High Templar Seren, and later raised by CICERO WINDBLADE. An Aasimar blessed with celestial heritage, he took an oath of vengeance and became a disciple of LEVIATHAN.

He sought knowledge of his god and his fallen angelic guardians, questing to return to his homeland. He wielded a holy amulet of Leviathan.

## The Aeternum Crisis

ZADSHEN's journey through the Crisis was marked by divine revelations and heroic sacrifice:

### Key Moments

- **The Council of the Wood** - Rose and gave a rousing speech proclaiming the need for the great houses to unite against evil
- **The Catacombs** - Drank a polymorphing potion, transforming into a young black dragon and attacking the party before being subdued
- **Battle with Neerius** - Charged the ancient vampire lord, landing devastating power smites. Was brought down by vargheists who tore out his throat, only to be revived by ARCA
- **The King Wish** - Used a Luck Blade to wish for King BEREN to abdicate his throne, with unknown consequences for altering Fate

### Divine Visions

LEVIATHAN showed him visions of:
- The link between angel souls and demon souls
- An ancient alliance between Celestials and HOUSE AETHERIUS
- A vision of a celestial kingdom spanning the continent
- The Qunari, the ancient order his bloodline has ties to

## Communion with His Blade

ZADSHEN communed with his blade and learned:
- The blade's will to only battle the strongest
- He leaped from a volcano in a leap of faith
- Learned how to become the champion of Leviathan
- Needed 1000 followers, a cleric and a paladin to fully manifest Leviathan on the material plane

## Post-Crisis Fate

After the Crisis, ZADSHEN was rescued from 17 years of planar torture and retired to MOUNT CELESTIA, the celestial plane of lawful good, where he serves as an acolyte in eternal service to the divine.

## Abilities

- **Divine Smite** - Channel divine energy through weapon strikes
- **Aura of Protection** - Shield allies with divine grace
- **Vow of Enmity** - Mark enemies for destruction
- **Angelic Wings** - Manifest glowing celestial wings
- **Lay on Hands** - Divine healing through touch

## Relationships

- **CICERO WINDBLADE** - Adoptive mentor
- **LEVIATHAN** - Divine patron
- **The Fellowship** - Brothers bound by fate
- **SHO** - Their fates were revealed to be intertwined
