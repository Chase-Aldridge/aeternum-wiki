# KISHIMOTO DRACONBLADE

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Master of the Soul Washers |
| **Race** | Dragonborn |

## Description

KISHIMOTO DRACONBLADE became the new master of THE SOUL WASHERS with BENGROR THE MIGHTY as his right hand sword.

The DRACONISS dragonborn founded their own island village named WAJA-SAN to honor their sensei and his peaceful death in 1112AE. KISHIMOTO began training the next generation in the ways of Dragon Bushido.
