# ODA

**Full Name:** Oda the Monk
**Race:** Human
**Class:** Monk (Way of the Open Hand)
**Affiliation:** [The Fellowship of the Blade](/docs/aeternum/characters/the-fellowship)

## Background

ODA is a disciplined human monk who follows the Way of the Open Hand. A master of martial arts and inner peace, he serves as one of the core members of the Rusitauri fellowship.

After the Aeternum Crisis, ODA returned to DARGO and the temple of THE CONIN with LUAN BO to train a new generation and craft his own personal martial art.
