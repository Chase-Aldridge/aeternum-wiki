# THE PANTOKRATOR

**Title:** Illithid Lord
**Race:** Illithid

## Description

THE PANTOKRATOR is an Illithid Lord of immense psionic power.

This mind flayer participated in the titanic duel at MAGUS RISE alongside BLACK FANG and NERO VALUS. The ruins are now declared off-limits officially by the major kingdoms.
