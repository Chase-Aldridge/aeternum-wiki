# LUAN BO "The Celestial Tiger"

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Master of Da'Shon |
| **Race** | Human |

## Description

LUAN BO "The Celestial Tiger" is a legendary martial artist who refounded a dojo and temple to THE CONIN in DARGO.

Starting in 1112AE, he began training the next generation in his way Da'Shon alongside ODA of the Rusitauri.
