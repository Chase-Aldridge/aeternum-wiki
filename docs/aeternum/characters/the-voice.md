# THE VOICE

*Ascended Demigod, Leader of The Guild*

## Overview

**Title:** The Voice
**Race:** Unknown (Ascended)
**Status:** Demigod
**Affiliation:** The Guild
**Domain:** Shadow Citadel in Limbo
**Role:** Continental arbiter and Guild leader

## Ascension

THE VOICE is the newest demigod to walk the material plane, having ascended during the tumultuous events of the Aeternum Crisis. The exact nature of their ascension remains shrouded in mystery, but their power is undeniable.

## Post-Crisis Role

After the fall of NEJUNE and the defeat of the old demigods, THE VOICE emerged as a stabilizing force:

### Political Power
- Assisted the Fellowship as a member of THE GUILD council
- Brokered a new, albeit tenuous, geopolitical peace on AETERNUM
- Helped guide the newly formed council to stabilize the continent
- Oversaw rebuilding of MERIA, DARGO, and THANE'S HOLD

### The Shadow Citadel
THE VOICE rules from the SHADOW CITADEL in LIMBO:
- Formerly the headquarters of the slain god KET
- Now serves as their throne and seat of power
- A nexus point between planes
- Repository of ancient knowledge and artifacts

## Relationship with the Fellowship

THE VOICE granted the heroes considerable rewards:
- Political protection and influence
- Financial resources
- Established their staff, followers, and equipment within the GUILD tower in MERIA
- Provided access to the Shadow Citadel itself

## Powers

As an ascended demigod, THE VOICE possesses:

- **Divine Magic** - Powers beyond mortal understanding
- **Planar Travel** - Move between realms at will
- **Truth Sight** - See through all deception
- **Continental Influence** - Shape events across Aeternum
- **Immortality** - Beyond mortal death

## The Guild Connection

Under THE VOICE's leadership, THE GUILD has transformed:
- From mercenary organization to continental government
- Maintains peace through diplomatic and military means
- Acts as neutral arbiter between the great houses
- Protects the realm from external threats
