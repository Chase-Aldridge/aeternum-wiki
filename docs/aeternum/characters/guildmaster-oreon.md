# GUILDMASTER OREON

**Title:** Warlord of the Twilight Reavers
**Race:** Unknown

## Description

GUILDMASTER OREON is one of the new warlords heading MERIA's elite mercenary force known as THE TWILIGHT REAVERS.

Along with LU ZHONG THE TIGER, GROMWINSTON THE GARRULOUS, and RYUZEN THE DRUNKEN PHOENIX, they amassed a large military and trade fleet and set sail north in 1114AE into the uncharted seas with the blessing of THE VOICE using old maps from the dawn era.
