# ULRICK BLAZE

*Human Warlock, Former Paladin of Silvanus*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Ulrick Blaze |
| **Race** | Human |

| **Affiliation** | The Fellowship of the Blade |
| **Patrons** | Silvanus (former), Oberon (pact) |
| **Familiar** | Little Leaf (fey sprite) |
| **Status** | Soul sealed in adamantine jar in Bytopia |

## Background

ULRICK was born during a lightning storm in a small community deep in THE FELLWOOD. Taught by THALINDRA, he lost his sister and family to Lord TARTULIEN, an AETHERIUS baron. He left his home seeking revenge and to forge a new balance between nature and civilization.

He wielded a crystal pendant of protection charged with lightning power by THALINDRA herself.

## The Fall and Pact

ULRICK's story is one of temptation and consequences:

### The Warlock Pact
- Met the warlock DRELL who served OBERON, King of the Feywild
- Made a pact with OBERON in exchange for power
- Gained wild magic abilities with unpredictable effects
- Was tasked with missions that tested his morality

### Wild Magic Consequences
ULRICK's power came at terrible cost:
- Rapidly aged 18+ years from wild magic surges
- Hair turned long, thin, and stark white
- Skin showed signs of blight and plague
- Form shrank nearly two feet
- Lips exploded with plagueborn magic

### Death and Resurrection
In a battle against AETHERIUS soldiers:
- ULRICK fell unconscious from spear wounds
- Was slain when a soldier drove a spear through his skull
- ARCA called upon divine intervention from ALAYA
- Was resurrected, but physically weakened forever

## Key Moments

### The Bone Company Massacre
Mad with power, ULRICK:
- Cast modify memory on Captain Gorn
- Unleashed wild magic killing his own horse
- Brought down sunburst flames on soldiers
- Helped massacre the entire company
- Was confronted by ARCA and ZADSHEN about his darkness

### Oberon's Missions
- Tasked with eliminating NEBULOS, YON'TAL's enforcer
- Sought to broker alliance between Feywild and Fellwood
- Worked to establish a permanent portal

## Post-Crisis Fate

When OBERON's power broke, ULRICK's soul escaped to BYTOPIA, a plane of industrious good. His soul now resides sealed in an adamantine jar, neither truly alive nor dead, awaiting some future redemption or release.

## Abilities

- **Wild Magic** - Unpredictable magical surges
- **Globe of Invulnerability** - Protective magic sphere

## Relationships

- **THALINDRA** - Mentor who died of the Blight
- **OBERON** - Archfey patron and manipulator
