# THANE'S HOLD

*The Dwarven Mountain Fortress*

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Dwarven Kingdom / Mountain Fortress |
| **Ruler** | The Merchant Guild and Thane Council |
| **Population** | Large dwarven population |
| **Status** | Fell to the Red Horde, retreated to Underdark |

## Description

THANE'S HOLD was the greatest dwarven stronghold in Aeternum, a massive fortress-city carved into the mountains. Its fall to THE RED HORDE marked one of the darkest moments of the Aeternum Crisis.

## The Siege

### The Fall
- Upper gates besieged by THE RED HORDE
- Lower gates leading to UNDERDARK also threatened
- Giant king TAUREN ROCKBREAKER led overwhelming siege army
- THE GRAY GATE fell first, cutting off reinforcements

### Desperate Measures
The dwarves:
- Awakened ancient rune golems to defend their halls
- Prepared death runes to collapse the mountain
- Made fighting retreat into the UNDERDARK
- Believed to have detonated the runes, destroying their beloved home

## Key Figures

### LIONEL BLACKPEBBLE
Representative at the Council of the Wood:
- Pleaded for help to lift the siege
- Scoffed at the inaction of other houses
- Honored the Fellowship's bravery

### BRAX BJORNSON
Merchant captain who:
- Offered the Fellowship jobs as sworn guards
- Provided introduction to THE MERCHANT GUILD
- Had troubled past with BRORNOR

## The Merchant Guild

A powerful dwarven economic force:
- Controlled trade through THE GRAY WASTES
- Offered refuge contracts worth 11,000GP per year
- Could seal their gates for up to a decade
- Economic partner with HOUSE AETHERIUS

## Military Assets

- Heavily fortified upper and lower gates
- Ancient rune golems (awakened in desperation)
- Death runes capable of collapsing the mountain
- Professional dwarven warriors

## Legacy

The fall of THANE'S HOLD:
- Created economic crisis for AETHERIUS
- Ended land trade across the continent
- Forced refugees to flee to MERIA and beyond
- Demonstrated the power of THE RED HORDE
- The dwarves' fate in the UNDERDARK remains unknown
