:::warning Partially Fallen

:::

## Description

The last standing dwarven cityhold on the continent. A fortress containing a small but stubborn clan of underground nobility.

The craftsmen and tinkerers are held in the **highest regard** for their work with:
- Weaponcraft and armorcraft
- Jewels and artifacts
- **Aeternum Ore** mining and shaping

## City Structure

### Upper Mountain (Fallen)
- Massive fortified gate
- Trader access areas
- **Thane's Merchant Guild** headquarters

### Deep Levels
Hidden from outsiders:
- **The Throne Room**
- Barracks and armories
- Mines and forges
- Secret defensive positions

## Political Status

Traditionally a **neutral city** during house warfare. However, due to frequent orc and goblin incursions, they signed secret defensive pacts with unknown parties.

## Current Status (1116 AE)

- Upper holds slowly being retaken with Guild and Meria assistance
- Brutal war of attrition against Red Horde remnants
- Plutocracy of merchant guilds under **High King Thanor** in ascendancy
- Reopened trade, returning to top economic monopolies on forged goods and ores

## The Slayerwatch

A notorious minor clan of dwarven barbarians whose sole purpose is to **die gloriously in battle**. Leader **Hralfgar the Black** is infamous among the orcs for taking heads and keeping score.

- Power Rating: 1/10
- Alignment: Chaotic Good
- Status: PRESUMED MISSING

## Thane's Merchant Guild

Responsible for 200 years of wealth and prosperity through political and mercantile expertise.

Major exports:
- Ore
- Weapons and armor
- Artifacts and smithing tools
- Jewelry
