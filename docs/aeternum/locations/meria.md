# MERIA

*The Merchant Kingdom, Seat of The Guild*

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Major Kingdom / City-State |
| **Ruler** | The Guild Council (led by OREON) |
| **Population** | Large (hundreds of thousands) |
| **Primary Economy** | Trade, Banking, Mercenary Contracts |
| **Notable Location** | THE LAUGHING DRAGON tavern |

## Description

MERIA is the commercial heart of Aeternum, a bustling port city where gold speaks louder than noble blood. The city thrives on trade from across the continent and serves as the headquarters of THE GUILD, the most powerful mercenary and merchant organization in the realm.

## Key Locations

### THE LAUGHING DRAGON
The famous tavern where the Fellowship of the Blade first met. Run by:
- **BRORNOR BATTLEHAND** - Venerable dwarven barkeep and Guild contact
- **YARIN** - Silver dwarf innkeeper, second-in-command
- **GROMWINSTON THE GARRULOUS** - Famous gnome bard in residence

### The Guild Tower
Located on an isle in the port district, this is where:
- Ambassadors and mercenaries seek contracts
- The Guild lords hold court
- International trade is negotiated
- The Fellowship established their base post-Crisis

### Other Notable Sites
- **The Trade Quarter** - Where "THE FENCE" operates
- **The Great Library** - Where ARCHIVIST BELVAS assists researchers
- **The Slaver's Market** (historical) - Now abolished

## Political Situation

During the Aeternum Crisis:
- Economic battle with AETHERIUS over trade routes
- Sea trade increased between MERIA and DARGO
- Refugee crisis as peoples fled the war
- Sent regiments to reinforce THE GRAY GATE
- Secretly negotiated with NEJUNE lords

## Military

- Professional city guard with doubled patrols during crisis
- Naval fleet (suffered losses to storm siege giants)
- Mercenary contracts through THE GUILD
- Cannot commit ground forces through GRAY WASTES

## Economy

- Lucrative trade with THANE'S HOLD (disrupted by siege)
- Banking and financial services
- Mercenary contract fees
- Port and shipping taxes
