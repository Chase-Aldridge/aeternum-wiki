## Notable Establishments

- **The Laughing Dragon** (Meria) - Famous tavern where the Fellowship formed
- **The Guild Tower** (Meria) - Headquarters of The Guild
- **Temple of the Conin** (Dargo) - Martial arts dojo run by Luan Bo and Oda
- **Waja-San** (Emerald Isles) - Dragonborn village of the Soul Washers
