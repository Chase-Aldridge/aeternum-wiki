# DARGO

*The Eastern Kingdom, Land of Warriors*

## Overview

**Type:** Major Kingdom
**Ruler:** The noble houses (historically House Dragonspear)
**Population:** Large
**Primary Culture:** Martial traditions, Honor codes
**Religion:** Temples to multiple gods including Leviathan

## Description

DARGO is the eastern kingdom of Aeternum, known for its strong martial traditions and warrior culture. The land breeds fighters, paladins, and those who live by codes of honor. It is also the ancestral home of ZADSHEN DRAGONSPEAR.

## Key Locations

### The Temple of THE CONIN
Where ODA returned after the Crisis to train new martial artists alongside LUAN BO. A center for monk traditions and eastern fighting styles.

### Leviathan's Temple
ZADSHEN learned that LEVIATHAN wished his temple be built in DARGO, connecting the kingdom to the sea god's power.

### THE GILDED DRAGON
Great market and tavern where the heroes met rogues and traded for powerful items like THE HELM OF THE IRONBREAKER.

## Political Situation

### The Dragonspear Claim
ZADSHEN revealed his DRAGONSPEAR lineage, claiming potential right to the throne. His "King Wish" using the Luck Blade caused King BEREN to call him "usurper."

### Alliance Politics
- Historical alliance with HOUSE AETHERIUS
- LADY MERA represented DARGO at councils
- Fleet stood ready but would not sail north during the Crisis
- THE SILVER SENTINELS sent by SER LORA to investigate NEJUNE

## Military Forces

- Professional knights and warriors
- Naval fleet under LADY MERA's command
- THE SILVER SENTINELS - Elite investigative force
- Strong defensive fortifications

## Culture

### Martial Traditions
- Samurai-influenced warrior codes
- Monk temples and training grounds
- Dueling culture among nobles
- YURIO ZARASHI known to challenge knights here

### The Dragonborn Connection
- SHO aided the DARGO fleet in capturing WARDEN ships
- Rescued dragonborn slaves
- Connected scattered dragonborn communities

## Notable Figures

- **LADY MERA** - Noble representative
- **SER LORA** - Sent the Silver Sentinels
- **King BEREN** - Current ruler (contested by Zadshen's wish)
- **High Templar SEREN** - Zadshen's late surrogate father
