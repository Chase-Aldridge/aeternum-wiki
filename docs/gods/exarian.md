# Exarian, the Titan of Light

**Type:** Titan
**Alignment:** Unknown
**Domains:** Light, Matter, Creation

## Description

First and oldest of the Titans, Exarian created light and matter itself.

He has never been described or seen in all the annals of the ancient elves and dwarves of Lor. He is believed to sit atop his own plane above all creation writing in **THE WORLDTOME**, a book of metareality that already contains past, present, and future.
