# Gods & Pantheon

The divine beings that shape the World of LOR. From the primordial Titans who created existence to the ascended demigods of Aeternum.

---

## The Three Titans of Creation

*The first beings that incarnated in the multiverse, creating the planes, the planet Lor, and all other gods.*

| Titan | Domain | Role |
|-------|--------|------|
| [Exarian](/docs/gods/exarian) | Light, Matter, Creation | The Creator - shaped the physical world |
| [Kronus](/docs/gods/kronus) | Time, Mortality, Death | The Timekeeper - governs the cycle of life |
| [Alariel](/docs/gods/alariel) | Magic, The Weave | The Weaver - source of all arcane power |

---

## Demigods of Aeternum

*Mortal-born beings who ascended to divinity*

### The Silent Six
Six demigods who fell during the Aeternum Crisis of 1111 AE. Their power has been absorbed or scattered.

### The Voice
The newest demigod, ascended during the Aeternum Crisis. Now leads The Guild from the Shadow Citadel in Limbo.

## Divine Hierarchy

```
TITANS (Primordial)
├── Exarian, Kronus, Alariel
│
GREATER GODS (Cosmic)
├── Bahamut, Tiamat, Pelor, etc.
│
LESSER GODS (Planar)
├── Leviathan, Eideon, etc.
│
DEMIGODS (Ascended Mortals)
├── The Voice, The Silent Six (fallen)
│
FEY POWERS (Otherworldly)
└── Oberon, Titania, Baba Yaga, etc.
```
