# Tiamat

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Chaotic Evil
**Domains:** Death, Nature, Tempest

## Description

Female goddess of chaos who appears as a terrible five-headed dragon.

Chiefly worshiped by TAMARYEN yuan-ti and evil dragonborn.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Wakizashi |
| **Holy Colors** | White, Black, Green, Blue, and Red |
| **Offerings** | Parchment, ash, or music |
| **Divine Plane** | Draconium (The Abyss) |
