# TIAMAT

*Goddess of Chaos*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Evil |
| **Domains** | Death, Nature |
| **Weapon** | Wakizashi |
| **Colors** | White, Black, Green, Blue, and Red |
| **Offerings** | Parchment, ash, or music |
| **Plane** | Draconium (The Abyss) |

---

## Description

Tiamat is the **female goddess of chaos**, appearing as a terrible five-headed dragon - each head representing one of the chromatic dragon types.

---

## Worship

Tiamat is chiefly worshiped by:
- Evil dragonborn
- Chromatic dragons
- Cultists seeking power through chaos
- Those who oppose Bahamut

---

## In the Aeternum Crisis

Tiamat's influence was felt throughout the Crisis:
- She corrupted the Temple of Bahamut
- Her servants worked to spread chaos
- Dragons empowered by her attacked the heroes
- EDORA died whispering "Hail Tiamat"

Her influence was ultimately banished from the Temple when SHO became Bahamut's Champion.

---

## The Five Heads

Each of Tiamat's heads represents a different aspect:
- **White** - Cold and cunning
- **Black** - Corrosive and cruel
- **Green** - Poisonous and deceptive
- **Blue** - Lightning and tyranny
- **Red** - Fire and destruction

---

## Tiamat vs Bahamut

The eternal conflict between Tiamat (chaos) and Bahamut (order):
- They are eternal nemeses
- Their followers war perpetually
- The balance affects all dragonkind

---

*"From chaos comes power. From destruction comes renewal. Tiamat teaches the truth of fire."*
