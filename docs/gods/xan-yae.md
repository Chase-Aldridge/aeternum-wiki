# Xan Yae

**Type:** Lesser God
**Alignment:** True Neutral
**Domains:** Light, Knowledge, Life, Peace

## Description

Female goddess of the unseen who appears as a slender, serene monk.

Chiefly worshiped by monks, clerics, and paladins.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Hand wraps |
| **Holy Colors** | Pure White and Grey |
| **Offerings** | Candles, scrolls, prayer wheels |
| **Divine Plane** | The Unseen Monastery (The Outlands) |
