# XAN YAE

*Goddess of the Unseen*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Alignment** | True Neutral |
| **Domains** | Light, Knowledge, Life, Peace |
| **Weapon** | Hand wraps |
| **Colors** | Pure White and Grey |
| **Offerings** | Candles, scrolls, prayer wheels |
| **Plane** | The Unseen Monastery (The Outlands) |

---

## Description

Xan Yae is the **female goddess of the unseen**, appearing as a slender, serene monk - the embodiment of inner peace and hidden wisdom.

---

## Worship

Xan Yae is chiefly worshiped by:
- Monks seeking enlightenment
- Clerics of the contemplative orders
- Paladins who value peace over war
- Those who seek hidden knowledge

---

## Philosophy

Xan Yae's teachings emphasize:
- Inner peace and serenity
- Knowledge through contemplation
- The power of the unseen
- Balance in all things

---

## The Unseen Monastery

Her celestial home in the Outlands serves as:
- A place of eternal meditation
- Repository of hidden knowledge
- Training ground for the spirit

---

*"What the eye cannot see, the soul may know. Xan Yae teaches us to look beyond."*
