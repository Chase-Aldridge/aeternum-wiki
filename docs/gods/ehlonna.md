# Ehlonna

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Domains:** Nature, Life, Twilight

## Description

Female goddess of nature and fertility who appears as a beautiful elven priestess.

Chiefly worshiped by VOL'ANLEK and elves across GUL'RATH.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Staff, Bow |
| **Holy Colors** | Green, White |
| **Offerings** | Leaves, Branches, Flowers |
| **Divine Plane** | Verdant Boughs (Elysium) |
