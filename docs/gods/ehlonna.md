# EHLONNA

*Goddess of Nature and Fertility*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Nature, Life, Twilight |
| **Weapon** | Staff, Bow |
| **Colors** | Green, White |
| **Offerings** | Leaves, Branches, Flowers |
| **Plane** | Verdant Boughs (Elysium) |

---

## Description

Ehlonna is the **female goddess of nature and fertility**, appearing as a beautiful elven priestess - the embodiment of natural grace and life.

---

## Worship

Ehlonna is chiefly worshiped by **elves** across Lor:
- The high elves of VOL'ANLEK
- Wood elves of the forests
- Any who revere nature's beauty

Her worship is also popular among:
- Druids
- Rangers
- Those who tend gardens and forests

---

## In Gul'Rath

Commander Striker Horun lists Ehlonna among his patron deities, representing the elven connection to the natural world even for warriors.

---

## Domains

### Nature
Ehlonna governs forests, animals, and the wild places of the world.

### Life
As goddess of fertility, she blesses births, harvests, and the continuation of life.

### Twilight
The gentle transition between day and night falls under her domain.

---

*"Where forests grow and creatures thrive, Ehlonna's blessing is present."*
