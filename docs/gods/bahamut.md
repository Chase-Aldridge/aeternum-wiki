# Bahamut

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Lawful Good
**Domains:** Nature, War

## Description

Male god of order who appears as a great silver dragon.

Chiefly worshiped by goodly dragonborn, some knights, and yuan-ti. It is dreamt by many prophets that BAHAMUT and the other metallic dragon gods have chosen not to show themselves yet to the peoples of AETERNUM as they did in the dawn era.
