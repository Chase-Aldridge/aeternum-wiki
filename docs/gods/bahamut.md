# BAHAMUT

*God of Order and Justice*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Good |
| **Domains** | Nature, War |
| **Weapon** | Dai-Katana |
| **Colors** | Pure Silver |
| **Offerings** | Ice, flowers, or incense |
| **Plane** | Platinum Keep (Arcadia) |

---

## Description

Bahamut is the **male god of order**, appearing as a great silver dragon - the paragon of draconic virtue and justice.

---

## Worship

Bahamut is chiefly worshiped by:
- Goodly dragonborn
- Certain orders of knights
- Paladins seeking justice
- Those who oppose Tiamat's chaos

---

## Champion of Bahamut

During the Aeternum Crisis, **SHOREA (SHO)** became the Champion of Bahamut:
- Journeyed to THE ISLE OF THE DRAGON
- Entered the soul of his sword and learned its true name: **REN**
- Underwent trials in an ocean of combat
- Unlocked **Shikai** and **Bankai**
- Received Bahamut's blessing
- Banished Tiamat's influence from the temple

---

## The Metallic Dragon Mystery

Following the Crisis, Bahamut and the other goodly metallic dragon gods have chosen **not to show themselves** to the peoples of Aeternum. The reason remains unknown.

Many aspirants seek refuge in the TEMPLE OF BAHAMUT to await his return.

---

## Bahamut vs Tiamat

The eternal conflict between Bahamut (order) and Tiamat (chaos) shapes much of draconic religion:
- They are eternal nemeses
- Their followers are perpetually opposed
- The balance between them affects the world

---

*"Where there is injustice, Bahamut's gaze falls. Where there is tyranny, his wrath follows."*
