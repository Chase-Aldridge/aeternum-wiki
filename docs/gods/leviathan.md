# Leviathan

**Type:** Lesser God
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Domains:** Tempest, Light

## Description

Male god of balance who appears as a great blue dragon.

Chiefly worshiped by aasimar and celestials.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Lightning |
| **Holy Colors** | Blue, White, Gold |
| **Offerings** | Feathers |
| **Divine Plane** | Cerulean Sanctum (Arborea) |
