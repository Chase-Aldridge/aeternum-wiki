# Gaia

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Domains:** Nature, Life, Light

## Description

Female goddess of life and nature itself who appears as all living things.

Chiefly worshiped by those who dwell in wild places.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Storms |
| **Holy Colors** | Green, White |
| **Offerings** | Pebbles, seeds, dew |
| **Divine Plane** | The Core (Elysium) |
