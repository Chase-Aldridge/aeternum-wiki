# GAIA

*Goddess of Life and Nature*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Nature, Life, Light |
| **Weapon** | Storms |
| **Colors** | Green, White |
| **Offerings** | Pebbles, seeds, dew |
| **Plane** | The Core (Elysium) |

---

## Description

Gaia is the **female goddess of life and nature itself**. She appears as all living things - the embodiment of the natural world in all its forms.

---

## Worship

Gaia is chiefly worshiped by:
- Those who dwell in wild places
- Druids and rangers
- Farmers and gardeners
- Anyone who reveres the natural world

---

## Domains

### Nature
Gaia governs all natural processes - growth, decay, seasons, weather, and the cycles of life.

### Life
As goddess of life itself, Gaia's power extends to healing, birth, and the vital force that animates all creatures.

### Light
The light of the sun that enables growth falls under Gaia's domain.

---

## Relationship to Plagas

In the wild continent of PLAGAS, Gaia's influence is particularly strong. The untamed jungles and their incredible biodiversity are seen as expressions of her power.

---

*"All that lives, lives through Gaia. All that grows, grows by her will."*
