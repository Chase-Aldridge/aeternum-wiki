# GRUNGNAR

*God of Smithing and Courage*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Good |
| **Domains** | War |
| **Weapon** | Axe |
| **Colors** | Red and Brown |
| **Offerings** | Carved stone or refined ore |
| **Plane** | Dwarfhelm (Mount Celestia) |

---

## Description

Grungnar is the **male god of smithing and courage**, appearing as a stout, enraged dwarf - the epitome of dwarven martial spirit.

---

## Worship

Grungnar is chiefly worshiped by **all dwarves** across Lor:
- Smiths invoke his name when forging
- Warriors pray to him before battle
- Miners ask his blessing for courage in the depths

---

## In Gul'Rath

The dwarves of IRONHELM are devoted followers of Grungnar. Emperor Aldrick Ironshield and his people honor the god through:
- Excellence in smithing
- Courage in battle
- Defense of their mountain homes

---

## In Plagas

Even the duergar of Fort Runestone pay homage to Grungnar, though their interpretation of his teachings is... darker.

---

## The Dwarven Way

Grungnar embodies the core dwarven virtues:
- **Craftsmanship** - Creating works of lasting quality
- **Courage** - Facing danger without flinching
- **Determination** - Never surrendering, never yielding

---

*"A dwarf with an axe and Grungnar's blessing fears nothing."*
