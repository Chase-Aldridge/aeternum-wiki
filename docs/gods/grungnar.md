# Grungnar

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Domains:** War, Forge

## Description

Male god of smithing and courage who appears as a stout enraged dwarf.

Chiefly worshiped by all dwarves in the DRAGONSPINE MOUNTAINS and across all dwarven lands.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Axe |
| **Holy Colors** | Red and Brown |
| **Offerings** | Carved stone or refined ore |
| **Divine Plane** | Dwarfhelm (Mount Celestia) |
