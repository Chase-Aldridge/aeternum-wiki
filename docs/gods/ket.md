# Ket (SLAIN)

**Type:** Lesser God
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral
**Domains:** Trickery, Knowledge

## Description

Male god of commerce and gold who appeared as an opulent cloaked lord.

Was chiefly worshiped by DRAGMAR AER. Slain during the events leading to the Aeternum Crisis. His former headquarters, THE SHADOW CITADEL in LIMBO, now serves as the throne for THE VOICE.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Dagger |
| **Holy Colors** | Orange and Blue |
