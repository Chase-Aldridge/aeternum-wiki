# ALARIEL, TITAN OF MAGIC

*Third of the Three Titans of Creation*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Mythological / Believed to Exist |
| **Role** | Creator of Magic |
| **Rank** | Titan (Above Gods) |
| **Forms** | Many names and forms in every culture |
| **Plane** | Unknown |

---

## The Third Titan

Alariel is the **third and youngest** of the Titans - the primordial beings that created the multiverse, planes, and gods.

---

## Creation

Alariel created:
- **The Weave** - The underlying fabric of magic
- **The Winds of Magic** - The flows of arcane power
- **Spellcraft** - Magic in its primordial form

---

## The Protector

Alariel was key in helping her older brother Exarian in:
- Creating the various **pantheons of gods** to rule over their domains
- **Protecting the gods** from the devouring appetite of Kronos

She serves as a guardian against Kronos's destructive nature.

---

## Many Forms

Alariel has **many forms and names** in every culture across Lor:
- Different civilizations know her by different names
- She appears in various guises
- Her worship takes many forms

Yet her **planar intercessions are rare** - direct intervention from the Titan of Magic is almost unheard of.

---

## Worship

Unlike her darker sibling Kronos, Alariel is generally seen as:
- Benevolent to magic users
- Protective of creation
- Distant but not malevolent

---

## Relationship with Other Titans

| Titan | Relationship |
|-------|--------------|
| **Exarian** | Elder sibling; Together they created the gods |
| **Kronos** | Elder sibling; Actively protects creation from him |
