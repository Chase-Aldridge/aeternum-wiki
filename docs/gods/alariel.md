# Alariel, Titan of Magic

**Type:** Titan
**Alignment:** Unknown
**Domains:** Magic, The Weave

## Description

Third and youngest of the Titans, Alariel created the weave and winds of magic and spellcraft in its primordial form.

She was key in helping her older brother Exarian in creating the various pantheons of gods to rule over their domains, while also protecting them from the devouring appetite of Kronus.

She has many forms and names in every culture, and yet her planar intercessions are rare.
