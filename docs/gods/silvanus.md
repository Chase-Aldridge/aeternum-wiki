# Silvanus

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Domains:** Nature, Life

## Description

Male god of wild nature who appears as a sylvan forest avatar.

One of the oldest gods having arisen from the soul of nature itself on the planet. Chose AENARION THE HAWK of AETERNUM as his avatar in 1111AE.

Chiefly worshiped by leaderless elves, halflings, gnomes, and humans.
