# EIDEON

*God of the Sea*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Neutral |
| **Domains** | Nature, Tempest, Arcana |
| **Weapon** | Trident |
| **Colors** | Deep Blue and Indigo |
| **Offerings** | Shells, sand, sea water |
| **Plane** | Deep Seahome (The Beastlands) |

---

## Description

Eideon is the **male god of the sea**, appearing as an aquatic elven lord - master of the deep waters and all that dwells within.

---

## Worship

Eideon is chiefly worshiped by:
- Sea elves
- Sailors of all races
- Those who make their living from the ocean
- Coastal communities

---

## In Gul'Rath

The sea elves of VOL'ANLEK honor Eideon as their primary deity, and his influence extends to all who sail the waters around the continent.

---

## Domains

### Nature
Eideon governs all sea life and the oceanic ecosystem.

### Tempest
Storms at sea, waves, and the fury of the ocean fall under his domain.

### Arcana
The magical mysteries of the deep are Eideon's to command.

---

*"The sea gives life and takes it. Eideon decides which."*
