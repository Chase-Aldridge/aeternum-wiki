# Eideon

**Type:** Lesser God
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral
**Domains:** Nature, Tempest, Arcana

## Description

Male god of the sea who appears as an aquatic elven lord.

Chiefly worshiped by the sea elves and sailors of all races.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Trident |
| **Holy Colors** | Deep Blue and Indigo |
| **Offerings** | Shells, sand, sea water |
| **Divine Plane** | Deep Seahome (The Beastlands) |
