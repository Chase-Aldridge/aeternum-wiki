# Draconiss

**Type:** Lesser God
**Alignment:** True Neutral
**Domains:** Nature, Light

## Description

Male god of all dragonkin who appears as the first ancient wyrm.

Chiefly worshiped by all dragonborn and dragons - he is their all-father.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Katana |
| **Holy Colors** | Pure White |
| **Offerings** | Water or fire |
| **Divine Plane** | The World Tree (Beastlands) |
