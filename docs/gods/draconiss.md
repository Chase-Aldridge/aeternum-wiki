# DRACONISS

*God of All Dragonkin*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Alignment** | True Neutral |
| **Domains** | Nature, Light |
| **Weapon** | Katana |
| **Colors** | Pure White |
| **Offerings** | Water or fire |
| **Plane** | The World Tree (Beastlands) |

---

## Description

Draconiss is the **male god of all dragonkin**, appearing as the first ancient wyrm - the progenitor of all dragons.

---

## Worship

Draconiss is chiefly worshiped by **dragonborn** across Lor, particularly:
- The samurai of REGELUM
- The people of WAJA-SAN
- All who follow the ways of Dragon Bushido

---

## The Metallic Dragon Mystery

Following the Aeternum Crisis, prophets and seers who worship Draconiss (along with Bahamut and Leviathan) dream that the goodly metallic dragon gods have chosen **not to show themselves** as they did in the dawn era.

The reason remains unknown.

---

## The Temple of Bahamut

Many aspirants made for THE ISLE OF THE DRAGON and sought refuge within the guarded TEMPLE OF BAHAMUT to await the return of the dragon gods.

---

## Dragon Bushido

The martial philosophy followed by Draconiss's worshippers emphasizes:
- Honor and duty
- Mastery of the blade
- Balance between light and darkness
- Acceptance of one's nature

---
