# Pelor

**Type:** Greater God
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Domains:** Light, Life

## Description

Male god of the sun and agriculture who appears as the dawnfather.

The fastest growing cult on LOR currently, led by its chaplain paladins, prophetic clerics, and warm traders traveling far and wide.

Chiefly worshiped by humans and dwarves.

## Worship

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sacred Weapon** | Mace |
| **Holy Colors** | Yellow, Gold |
| **Offerings** | Sunlight, wheat |
| **Divine Plane** | The Dawn Sanctum (Elysium) |
