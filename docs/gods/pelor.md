# PELOR

*God of the Sun and Agriculture*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Status** | Greater God |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Light, Life |
| **Weapon** | Mace |
| **Colors** | Yellow, Gold |
| **Offerings** | Sunlight, wheat |
| **Plane** | The Dawn Sanctum (Elysium) |

---

## Description

Pelor is the **male god of the sun and agriculture**, appearing as the dawnfather - bringer of light and harvest.

---

## Worship

Pelor is chiefly worshiped by:
- Humans
- Dwarves
- Farmers and those who work the land
- Anyone who values the sun's blessing

---

## The Growing Faith

Following the end of the Aeternum Crisis, PELOR has gained a **large cult following** among the commoners in all kingdoms of AETERNUM.

This growth is due to the influx of traders worshiping in his name from GUL'RATH. The **fastest growing cult on LOR** is led by:
- Chaplain paladins
- Prophetic clerics
- Warm traders traveling far and wide

---

## Domains

### Light
Pelor governs sunlight and all its blessings.

### Life
As god of agriculture, he blesses harvests and ensures life continues.
