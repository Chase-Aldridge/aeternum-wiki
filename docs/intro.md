# The World of LOR

Welcome to the **World of LOR**, a D&D 5th Edition campaign setting created by Kevin Orosz.

## Overview

**LOR** is a super-continental planet approximately 130% larger than Earth, containing all major biomes and climates. The world is home to multiple continents, each with rich histories, complex political systems, and epic adventures waiting to unfold.

## The Continents

### [Aeternum](/docs/category/aeternum)
The primary continent where the original campaign took place. A land of four great noble houses locked in political intrigue, ancient magic, and the looming threat of the Red Horde. The events of 1111 AE, known as "The Aeternum Crisis," changed this continent forever.

### [Plagas](/docs/category/plagas)
A massive rainforest and desert archipelago to the far west. This wild frontier is home to leonin tribes, loxodon shamans, colonial settlements, and titanic beasts. The sequel campaign begins here in 1116 AE.

## The Fellowship of the Blade

The legendary heroes known as "The Rusitauri" (Sons of the Forest) who faced the Aeternum Crisis:

- **Shorea** - Silver Dragonborn Samurai, Champion of Bahamut
- **Zadshen Dragonspear** - Aasimar Paladin, Blessed of Leviathan
- **Ulrick Blaze** - Human Paladin, Champion of Oberon
- **Nynora** - Wood Elf Rogue
- **Arca Dawnbringer** - Human Cleric, Champion of Alaya
